# HealthifyMe

**HealthifyMe** is a hackathon prototype built around the **Health & Food** problem statement. The goal of this application is to help users make healthier food choices, track their eating habits, and receive personalized health-related insights in a simple and user-friendly way.

## 🚀 Overview

In today’s fast-paced lifestyle, people often struggle to maintain a healthy diet and monitor their nutrition. **HealthifyMe** is designed as a smart health and food assistant that bridges the gap between **food consumption** and **personal wellness** by providing an easy platform to manage food habits, nutrition, and health goals.

This prototype focuses on creating a digital solution that promotes:

* Healthy eating habits
* Better food awareness
* Personalized wellness support
* Convenient health tracking

## 🎯 Problem Statement

The hackathon problem statement was based on **Health and Food**, aiming to build a solution that can positively impact people’s lifestyle by improving the way they understand, choose, and consume food while also keeping their health in check.

## 💡 Solution

**HealthifyMe** acts as a health-focused food management platform where users can:

* Track meals and daily food intake
* Get nutritional information about food items
* Receive health-based recommendations
* Monitor wellness-related goals
* Build better eating habits with technology support

The application is designed to be simple, accessible, and useful for people who want to become more conscious about their food choices and overall health.

## ✨ Key Features

* **Food Tracking** – Log meals and keep track of daily food consumption
* **Nutrition Insights** – View basic nutritional details of foods
* **Health Recommendations** – Get suggestions based on eating patterns and health goals
* **User-Friendly Interface** – Simple and clean UI for smooth interaction
* **Prototype for Smart Wellness** – Built as a concept to combine health and food in one platform
